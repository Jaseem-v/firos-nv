Hello!

I’m Firosnv, an indian designer with 10+ years of experience and I have worked on a variety of in-house brands ranging from small nonprofits to large tech companies with ambitious goals. I have unique talent at seeing the big picture potential in a product or brand and then bringing it to life.

I always enjoy working on fast pace projects and love helping clients see their ideas come to life. My design styles are clean and minimal with emphasis on usability and ease of use. I have been involved in all the aspects of designing projects: from concept, to design, to testing and the implementation.

If you are looking for some help or advice for your project, then please feel free to send me a quick message and I will get back to you.
